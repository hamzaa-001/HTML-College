Pakistan Museum of Natural History

Pakistan Museum of Natural History is a public museum operated by the Pakistan Science Foundation. It was established in 1976 and is located at Shakarparian, Islamabad. The museum is divided into four different parts:
■ Botanical Sciences
■ Earth Sciences
■ Zoological Sciences
■ Public Services The exhibits and galleries display the ecology, geology, and archaeology of the country. It has more than 600,000 collections of antique objects. Moreover, they also show the fossils, stones, parts of various animals, and life-size images of extinct species. It is also considered to be a research center. Additionally, it organizes several workshops, lectures, and studies to teach society about the safety and classification of plants, animals, and mineral sources. All these are the reasons that make this museum one of the best historical places in Pakistan. If you want to visit it from another city, you can stay in any of the top hotels in Islamabad.
