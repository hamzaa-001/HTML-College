Golra Sharif Railway Museum

Golra Sharif Railway Museum is located near sector F-13, Islamabad. It is one of the most artifacts museums that date back to the beginning of railways in the subcontinent of the British colonial period. It was renovated in 2003 as a Golra Sharif Junction Railway Station, which combines the city with Peshawar.
